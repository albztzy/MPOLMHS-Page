1st Place - Science Fair 2023

Our school's Special Science Class won 1st place in the regional Science Fair 2023 with an innovative project focusing on renewable energy solutions. This achievement reflects the dedication and expertise of our students in the fields of science and technology.

Champion - Robotics Competition 2022

The Robotics team of Mayor Pablo O. Lim Memorial High School secured the championship in the 2022 Robotics Competition. The team developed a state-of-the-art robot designed for precision in industrial tasks, showcasing both technical skills and innovation.
