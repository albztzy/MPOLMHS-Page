Welcome to Mayor Pablo O. Lim Memorial High School!

Located in the picturesque town of Marawis, Valencia, Bohol, our school is dedicated to fostering a learning environment that empowers students to excel academically and develop essential life skills.

Our curriculum features specialized strands such as the **Science and Technology Strand**, **Technical-Vocational Livelihood (TVL)** programs in **Agriculture** and **Bread & Pastry**, and the **General Academic Strand**, preparing students for various career paths.

We also offer a **Special Science Class** and an **Enhanced Science Class with Robotics Integration**, ensuring students gain hands-on experience with the latest innovations.

With a faculty that includes **DOST-Scholars**, we are committed to nurturing curiosity, critical thinking, and creativity, preparing students for success in academics and beyond.

Join us as we inspire the next generation of leaders and innovators!
